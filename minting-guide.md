# Minting Guide

## Introduction
This guide explains how to mint the NFT on the Base network.

## Requirements
Before minting, make sure you have:

- A Web3 wallet (MetaMask or compatible)
- Base Mainnet added to your wallet
- Sufficient ETH on Base for minting and gas fees

## Step 1 – Connect Wallet
Visit the official mint page and connect your wallet using the “Connect Wallet” button.

## Step 2 – Select Quantity
Choose the number of NFTs you wish to mint (within allowed limits).

## Step 3 – Confirm Transaction
Approve the transaction in your wallet and wait for confirmation on Base.

## Step 4 – Verify Ownership
After confirmation, your NFT will appear in your wallet and on supported marketplaces.

## Important Notes
- Always verify you are using the official mint link.
- Never share your private keys or seed phrase.
- Gas fees may vary depending on network activity.
