# Whitelist Mint Guide

## Overview
This guide explains how eligible whitelist participants can mint the NFT before the public sale.

## Eligibility
Whitelist access is granted to selected wallets based on community participation and ecosystem involvement.

## Requirements
- A Web3 wallet (MetaMask or compatible)
- Base Mainnet configured
- Sufficient ETH on Base for mint price and gas

## Step 1 – Verify Eligibility
Connect your wallet to the official mint page.
If your wallet is whitelisted, the interface will allow minting.

## Step 2 – Select Mint Quantity
Whitelist participants may have a limited mint allocation per wallet.

## Step 3 – Confirm Transaction
Approve the transaction in your wallet and wait for on-chain confirmation.

## Important Notes
- Only use the official mint link.
- Whitelist spots are non-transferable unless otherwise specified.
- Do not share private keys or seed phrases.

## Network
Base Mainnet
